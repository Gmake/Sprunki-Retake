What is Sprunki Retake?

Sprunki Retake is a groundbreaking horror-themed music creation adventure game that combines the thrill of horror elements with creative music making. As a fan-made adaptation of the popular music-mixing game Incredibox, it offers players a unique blend of musical creativity and immersive gameplay.

How to Play

1. Choose Characters

Select from various characters, each representing unique sounds and abilities.

2. Mix Sounds

Combine different character sounds to create layered musical compositions.

3. Explore

Navigate through levels, discover hidden paths, and face unique challenges.

4. Share

Save your creations and share them with the Sprunki Retake community.
